What is Malaria?

Malaria is a disease that has the highest fatality rate in children. In 2022, there were 249 million malaria cases globally that led to 608,000 deaths in total. Of these deaths, 76 per cent were children under 5 years of age. That means that 462,080 children died from malaria in 2022.
